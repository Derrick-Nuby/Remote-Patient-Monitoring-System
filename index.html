Remote Patient Monitoring System

Save lives, Live long

Real-Time Health Monitoring

Our system continuously tracks vital signs, such as body temperature and heart rate, using state-of-the-art sensors. Stay informed about your health status in real-time.

Accurate and Reliable

We utilize the DS18B20 temperature sensor and the DY039 heart rate sensor to provide accurate and reliable health data. Trust in our technology to give you precise readings at all times.

Data Visualization

View your health trends with our easy-to-understand graphs and charts. Track changes in your vital signs over time and gain insights into your health.

how it works

Sensor Integration

Our app integrates with the DS18B20 temperature sensor and the DY039 heart rate sensor, which are easy to set up and use.

Data Collection

The sensors continuously collect data on your body temperature and heart rate, providing real-time updates.

Data Transmission

Your health data is transmitted to a secure server where it can be accessed by your healthcare provider for monitoring and analysis.
